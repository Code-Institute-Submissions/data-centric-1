WELCOME TO YOGIC RECIPES

Here you can view Positive Pranic food recipes, also known as the "Yogic" and "Sattvic" diet, which is a diet commonly adapted by yogis, sages and spirituals. The Yogic diet is split into the three food categories: Positive Pranic (Gives you energy), Zero Pranic (Makes you lethargic) and Negative Pranic(Takes your energy). The food is vegan with the exeption of honey which is regarded as highly benefical in the Yogic world.
